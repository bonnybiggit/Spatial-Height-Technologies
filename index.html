Who We Are

About Spatial Height Technologies

A forward-thinking technology company committed to solving complex business challenges through innovation.

Spatial Heights Technologies is a recently established geospatial solutions firm dedicated to advancing innovation in geographic information systems (GIS). We focus on delivering cutting-edge services, including geospatial solutions development, capacity building for organizations, surveying and mapping, and data collection and management and many more.

Headquartered in Nigeria, Spatial Heights aims to empower clients across various sectors by leveraging GIS technology to solve complex challenges, foster sustainable development, and drive data-driven decision-making. With a commitment to excellence and collaboration, the firm is poised to make a significant impact in the geospatial industry, particularly in West Africa.

Our Mission

To empower organizations with intelligent geospatial solutions that drive innovation, enhance decision-making, and unlock the power of location-based insights across Africa.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s leading geospatial company, recognized for driving innovation and enabling smarter, data-informed decisions.
